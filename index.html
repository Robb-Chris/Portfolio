About Me

I am Chris Robb, currently a Data Science Master's student at Tilburg University, The Netherlands. I graduated from Saxion University with a bachelor's degree in International Business Management in 2019/20 and proceeded to work as a Project Manager / Data Analyst for 3 years. Having found my passion for Data, I decided to pursue a Master's Degree in Data Science to broaden my programming, analytical and statistical knowledge.

Expertise

Having over 5 years of coding experience at work and in my personal time, I have explored several different areas listed in chronological order:

  • Web-oriented languages from HTML over PHP to Javascript
  • Hardware and embedded systems with C# and some C++
  • Data-oriented programming in Python and R
  • Databases and Query languages such as SQL, NoSQL with MongoDB, Azure, and SAP
  • Extensive working experience with Power BI and Qlik for Data Analytics as well as Power Automate and VBA automation

With my recent endeavour in Data Science and Engineering, I have broadened my Python knowledge in areas of Machine Learning and Image Analysis. In my 'Project Overview' below some of my bigger projects in those areas are highlighted and explained in more detail.
